Lumpia

- Side Dish Recipe By user-385 -

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground pork

  • 1 medium onion, chopped

  • 2 cloves garlic, minced

  • 1 medium carrot, grated

  • 1/2 cup green onions, chopped

  • 1 egg, beaten (for the filling)

  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce

  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper

  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch

  • Lumpia wrappers

  • 1 egg, beaten (for sealing)

  • Vegetable oil (for frying)

Procedure:

  1. In a bowl, mix the ground pork, onion, garlic, carrot, green onions, soy sauce, pepper, cornstarch, and beaten egg until well combined.

  2. Place a lumpia wrapper with one corner facing you. Put 1 to 2 tablespoons of filling near the bottom corner.

  3. Roll tightly, folding in the sides, and seal the edge with beaten egg.

  4. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Fry the lumpia in batches for 3-5 minutes or until golden brown and crispy.

  5. Drain on paper towels. Serve with your favorite dipping sauce, like sweet and sour or spicy vinegar.
