Bibingka

- Dessert Recipe By user-672 -

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups rice flour

  • 1/2 cup sugar

  • 1 tablespoon baking powder

  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

  • 1 1/2 cups coconut milk (or 1 can of coconut milk)

  • 1/4 cup water

  • 2 large eggs, beaten

  • 1/4 cup melted butter (or margarine)

  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

  • 1/2 cup grated cheddar cheese (for topping)

  • 2 salted eggs, sliced (for topping)

  • Banana leaves (for lining the pan)

Procedure:

  1. Lightly char the banana leaves over an open flame or in the oven for a few seconds to make them more pliable. Then line your baking pan with the banana leaves, trimming off any excess. This gives the bibingka a traditional flavor and aroma. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).

  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the rice flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt. Add the coconut milk, water, beaten eggs, melted butter, and vanilla extract. Mix well until smooth and fully combined. The batter should have a thick, smooth consistency.

  3. Pour the batter into your prepared pan, spreading it out evenly.

  4. Bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes or until the top is set and slightly golden. You can check by inserting a toothpick into the center—if it comes out clean, it's done.

  5. Once the bibingka has finished baking, remove it from the oven. While it's still warm, sprinkle the grated cheese on top and arrange the sliced salted eggs evenly. Return to the oven and bake for another 5-7 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.

  6. Once the bibingka is done, remove it from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes.
