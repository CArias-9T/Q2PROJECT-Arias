Crab and Corn Soup

- Side Dish Recipe By user-735 -

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb fresh crab (or 2-3 cups cooked crab meat)

  • 1/2 cup canned or fresh corn kernels

  • 1 medium onion, chopped

  • 2 cloves garlic, minced

  • 1 medium tomato, chopped

  • 4 cups chicken broth (or water)

  • 1 cup evaporated milk

  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce (or salt to taste)

  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper

  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil

  • 1/2 cup green onions, chopped (for garnish)

Procedure:

  1. If using fresh crab, boil and crack the crab shells to extract the meat. Set aside. (Alternatively, use pre-cooked crab meat.)

  2. In a large pot, heat the oil over medium heat. Saut√© the garlic, onion, and tomato until softened, about 3-4 minutes.

  3. Add the chicken broth (or water) and bring to a boil. Lower the heat and let it simmer for 10 minutes to blend the flavors.

  4. Add the corn, crab meat, and fish sauce (or salt). Simmer for another 5-7 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld.

  5. Stir in the evaporated milk, carrots (if using), and black pepper. Simmer for another 3-5 minutes until the soup is creamy and the carrots are tender.

  6. Ladle the soup into bowls and garnish with chopped green onions.
