Ensaladang Talong

- Side Dish Recipe By user-462 -

Ingredients:

  • 3 medium eggplants (preferably round or long variety)

  • 1 medium onion, chopped

  • 1 medium tomato, chopped

  • 1/2 small red onion, finely sliced

  • 1/4 cup fish sauce

  • 1 tablespoon vinegar

  • 1 tablespoon sugar

  • 1/4 teaspoon salt (adjust to taste)

  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper

  • 1 hard-boiled egg, sliced

  • 1/2 cup fresh chopped cilantro

Procedure:

  1. Grill the eggplants directly over a flame or on a grill pan until the skin is charred and the flesh is soft (about 10-15 minutes). Alternatively, you can broil them in the oven. Let them cool for a few minutes, then peel off the charred skin and discard the stems. Mash the flesh lightly with a fork.

  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the fish sauce, vinegar, sugar, salt, and pepper until the sugar dissolves.

  3. In a large bowl, combine the mashed eggplant, chopped tomato, and onion. Pour the dressing over the mixture and toss gently to combine.

  4. Finally, top with slices of hard-boiled egg and fresh cilantro. Serve chilled or at room temperature as a side dish.
