What is the Cookbook of Babel?

Welcome! This is the Cookbook of Babel, where your ideas come to life! On this website, you can submit as much recipes as your heart pleases, and upload them to our website. You can view other peoples recipes, and even different variations of recipes, all for free, on this website! If you would like to begin, check out "Submit Recipes".

What do we offer?


  • Unlimited recipes from the community

  • Many categories of dishes

  • The ability to post as much recipes you want

  • The ability to save other recipes

  • No subscriptions
