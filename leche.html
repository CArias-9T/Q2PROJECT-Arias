Leche Flan

- Dessert Recipe By user-499 -

Ingredients:

  • 10 large egg yolks

  • 1 can (14 oz) sweetened condensed milk

  • 1 can (12 oz) evaporated milk

  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

  • 1 cup granulated sugar (for the caramel)

Procedure:

  1. In a saucepan over medium-low heat, melt the sugar, stirring constantly, until it turns into a golden brown caramel. Be careful not to burn it. Once the sugar has melted and turned amber, quickly pour the hot caramel into a llanera (a small metal mold), flan mold, or any heatproof dish, swirling the pan to coat the bottom evenly. Let it cool and harden.

  2. In a mixing bowl, whisk the egg yolks gently. Be careful not to create too much foam. Add the sweetened condensed milk, evaporated milk, and vanilla extract. Mix until well combined, but avoid over-whisking to prevent bubbles in the custard.

  3. To ensure a smooth, silky texture, strain the mixture through a fine mesh sieve into another bowl to remove any bits of cooked egg or impurities.

  4. Pour the strained custard mixture into the caramel-coated mold. Place the flan mold in a steamer or pot, cover, and steam over medium heat for about 40-50 minutes. Check for doneness by inserting a toothpick or knife in the center. If it comes out clean, it's ready.

  5. Once done, remove the flan from the steamer and let it cool at room temperature. Refrigerate for at least 2-3 hours (or overnight) to allow the custard to set and develop its full flavor.

  6. To serve, run a knife around the edges of the mold to loosen the flan. Place a serving plate over the mold and invert it to release the flan. The caramel will drizzle over the custard.
