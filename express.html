Bicol Express

- Main Dish Recipe By user-738 -

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb pork belly, cut into small cubes

  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil

  • 1 medium onion, chopped

  • 3 cloves garlic, minced

  • 2-3 long red chilies, chopped (adjust to spice preference)

  • 3-4 Thai bird's eye chilies, chopped

  • 1 cup coconut milk

  • 1/2 cup coconut cream

  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce

  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper

  • Salt to taste

  • 1/2 cup water or chicken broth (if needed)

  • 1 tablespoon shrimp paste

  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar

Procedure:

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a large pan or wok over medium heat. Add the pork belly and cook until it’s browned and slightly crispy, about 8-10 minutes. Remove excess fat if necessary and set the pork aside.

  2. In the same pan, sauté the garlic, onion, and red chilies (both long and bird’s eye) until fragrant and softened, about 3-4 minutes.

  3. Stir in the shrimp paste, fish sauce, and ground black pepper. Let it cook for another 2 minutes.

  4. Pour in the coconut milk and coconut cream, and stir to combine. Add a pinch of salt and brown sugar to balance the flavors. Bring to a gentle simmer and cook for 10-12 minutes until the sauce thickens and the flavors meld.

  5. If the sauce becomes too thick, add water or chicken broth to achieve the desired consistency. Taste and adjust seasonings, adding more salt, fish sauce, or sugar as needed.

  6. Serve the Bicol Express hot with steamed rice. Garnish with extra sliced chilies if you like more heat.s
