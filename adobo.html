Adobo

- Main Dish Recipe By user-193 -

Ingredients:

  • 1 ½ lbs chicken (cut into serving pieces, bone-in or boneless)

  • 1 medium onion, sliced

  • 5 cloves garlic, minced

  • 2 bay leaves

  • 1/4 cup soy sauce

  • 1/4 cup vinegar

  • 1 teaspoon peppercorns

  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper

  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar

  • 1/2 cup water

  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil (for frying)

Procedure:

  1. In a bowl, combine the chicken, soy sauce, garlic, onion, bay leaves, and peppercorns. Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes (or up to 1 hour in the refrigerator) to allow the flavors to meld.

  2. Heat oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add the chicken pieces (reserving the marinade for later) and brown on all sides for about 5 minutes. This step adds depth to the flavor.

  3. Add the vinegar and let it cook for about 2 minutes (do not stir yet—this helps mellow the acidity of the vinegar). Pour in the reserved marinade, water, and brown sugar. Stir to combine.

  4. Bring to a simmer, then reduce the heat to low. Cover and simmer for about 30-40 minutes, until the chicken is cooked through and tender. Occasionally check the liquid level, adding a bit more water if necessary.

  5. Once the sauce has thickened slightly and the chicken is tender, taste and adjust seasoning (add more soy sauce, vinegar, or sugar if needed).
