Halo-Halo

- Dessert Recipe By user-221 -

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups shaved ice (or crushed ice)

  • 1/2 cup sweetened jackfruit (langka)

  • 1/2 cup sweetened saba bananas (or regular bananas, sliced and cooked with sugar)

  • 1/4 cup sweetened red beans (mung beans or adzuki beans, cooked and sweetened)

  • /4 cup coconut strings (niyog or macapuno)

  • 1/4 cup sweetened nata de coco

  • 1/4 cup sweetened kaong

  • 1/4 cup gulaman

  • 1/4 cup corn kernels

  • 1 scoop ube ice cream

  • 1 scoop leche flan

  • 1-2 tablespoons evaporated milk (for drizzling)

Procedure:

  1. In tall glasses or bowls, layer the sweetened fruits, beans, nata de coco, and other ingredients as desired. Start with a layer of jackfruit, followed by bananas, beans, corn, and jelly cubes. Then, fill the glass with shaved ice until it reaches the top.

  2. Add a scoop of ube ice cream on top of the shaved ice.

  3. Slice a piece of leche flan and place it on top of the ice cream.

  4. Drizzle the evaporated milk over the entire dessert to add creaminess.

  5. Serve immediately with a spoon and straw. Stir everything together before eating to combine all the sweet flavors and textures.
